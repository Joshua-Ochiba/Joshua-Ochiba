## 🧠 What I'm Building

### 🤖 Fit.AI — Your Smart Fitness Companion

A mobile-first fitness platform designed to guide users, track progress, and provide intelligent assistance through an AI-powered system.

Unlike typical fitness apps, Fit.AI focuses on **memory and personalization**, ensuring your progress, habits, and preferences are never lost.

#### 🔹 Platform Includes

* 📱 **Mobile App** – for workouts, tracking, and daily interaction
* 📊 **Admin Dashboard** – for monitoring users and analytics
* 💬 **Public Web Chatbot** – for accessible fitness guidance
